AWM

It is a bolt-action sniper rifle. A really strong sniper rifle used by many military forces. It also has a lot of variants like

  • AW (Arctic Warfare)
  • AWP (Arctic Warfare Police)
  • AWS (Arctic Warfare Supressed)
etc.
It has a stainless steel barrel, and depending on the cartridge, the length of the barrel changes too.
