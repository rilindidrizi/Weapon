SIG Sauer MPX Carbine

MPX is my favorite Mid-range weapon for many reasons. It looks really beautiful, really nice looking. The other reason is because it is powerful and it can shoot in a long distance (3700 m). Of course it is almost immposible to kill when you shoot from that far, but it does damage. Another reason why i love MPX Carbine so much is beacuse it uses 9X19 mm Parabellum bullets, and those bullets are not so expensive. You can get a box of 50-round online for about 10 dollars. It is used by armies of different countries like:

  • USA: US Army
  • Argentina: Law enforcement
  • India: Indian National security Guards
  • Thailand: Royal Thai Police.
This gun can also use .375 SIG and .40 S&W bullets.
