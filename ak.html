AK 47

AK 47, also known as Avtomat Kalashnikova is a gas-operated assault rifle produced by Soviet Union. It is a powerful gun and it is really good for close quarters because you can attach a bayonet in it. This gun is used in many places like:

  • Afganistan
  • Albania
  • Greece
  • Turkey,
and many more.
It also was used in many wars:
  • Vietnam War
  • Cambodian Civil War
  • Iraq-Iran War,
etc.
