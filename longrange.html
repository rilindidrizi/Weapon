Long range

Long range weapons are sniper rifles. Sniper rifles can shoot really far and you can see the target clearly even if the distance between you and target is so big. One thing you need to know if you want to shoot with sniper is that the bullet will have bullet drop, meaning that the bullet height will decrease with the distance. All you need to know is air humidity, wind direction and speed (because the wind can change the direction of the bullet).
