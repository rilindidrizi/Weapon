Beretta M9

The photo shown is the photo of a Beretta M9. This is a really powerful pistol. It was produced in Italy. Unfortunately this poweful gun was used in many wars. Some of them are:

  • Persian Gulf War
  • Somali Civil War
  • Kosovo War
  • Global War on Terrorism
    • War in Afganistan
    • Iraq War
It is a semiautomatic pistol used by United States Armed Forces. It was adopted by United States military as their service pistol in 1985. The M9 won the competition in the 1980s to replace the M1911A1 the primary sidearm of the U.S. military. It also defeated SIG P226 for cost reasons.
