M16

M16 is a family of military rifles adapted from the ArmaLite AR-15. It is really powerful. The proof for that is that it is used by United States Military. The original M16 has a magazine of 20-round. It has a newer adjustable rear sight, case deflector, heavy barrel, improved handguard, pistol grip and buttstock. It is equiped with a removable carrying handle and Picatinny rail for mountin optics and other ancillary devices. It is used by many armies of many different countries. The list of wars on which this gun was used is really big.
